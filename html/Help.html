    Frequently Asked Questions

    This website allows users to create new web pages by viewing and downloading ready-made templates. It's designed to help users build web pages quickly and efficiently using pre-designed layouts.

    • Home Page: Displays a collection of templates for users to view and explore.
    • About Page: Provides an overview of the website, including its goals, vision, and the team behind it.
    • Contact Page: Allows users to reach out to support for assistance or inquiries.
    • Templates Page: Shows all available templates, allowing users to view, explore, and download them.
    • Plans Page: Lists subscription options, including both monthly and yearly plans, for accessing premium templates and features.
    • Login Page: Enables users to log in or create a new account to access personalized features and content.
    • Help Page: Provides detailed assistance to users through FAQs and a contact form.

    In addition, users can click on their profile picture at the top right corner of the page to view and manage their profile.

    You can download a template from the "Templates" page by clicking on the "Download" button next to the template you want. This will send you to a new page then copy the code to use.

    The "Plans" page offers subscription options, including monthly and yearly plans. Subscribing gives you access to premium templates and additional features designed to enhance your web-building experience.

    You can reach out to our support team through the "Contact" page. Simply fill out the contact form with your details and message, or call our support line for immediate assistance.

    You can manage your profile by clicking on your profile picture in the top right corner of any page. This will take you to your profile page where you can update your information, view your downloads, and manage your subscription.
